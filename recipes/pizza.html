Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

Home

Description

Don't know what to eat on a Friday night and fancy something tasty? Well this is the recipe for you!

I love to make this pepperoni pizza as it is homemade, tasty, and some proper comfort food. See below for the ingredients and methods.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces pizza dough, store-bought or homemade
  • 1/2 cup of pizza sauce
  • 18-20 slices pepperoni
  • 12 ounces mozzerella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano
  • Flour for rolling and shaping dough

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500F.
  2. Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface. If it is hard to roll, let it rest on the side for 5 minutes so it can warm up. For a large pizza, roll it to 14inches in diameter.
  3. Transfer the dough to a lightly dusted pizza peel. Add the sauce in a light layer al over the pizza, leaving a quarter of an inch crust around the edges. Chop half of the pepperoni and sprinkle it over the sauce. Top the pizza with grated cheese and the rest of the pepperoni. Season with black pepper.

  4. If using a pizza stone, cook for 6 minutes and then rotate the pizza halfway so it cooks evenly. Cook for another 6-8 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and charred in spots.

    If using a skillet, press the dough into a cast iron skillet and add toppings. Place the skillet over a high heat burner for 2 minutes to get it preheated and get the crust cooking right away. Transfer to a 500F oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.

  5. Slice and serve!
